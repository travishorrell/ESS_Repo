Who We Are

We are small software company located in Idaho Falls ID. Founted in 2022 we started the company with the goal to make the best software in the industy for first responders.

We believe that first responders need the best tools and equipment to do their jobs. But these industery have been forgoten when it comes to technology. We provide software that can make one of the hardest jobs easier and safer. This is the goal we have when ever we built new systems and services.
